Who We Are

Absolute Marble and Granite is a full service fabricator and installer of customized natural and engineered stones. Our experienced team has completed thousands of projects including residential and commercials properties. We specialize in fabricating kitchen countertops, fireplaces, bar tops and vanities.


What We Do

At Absolute Marble and Granite, we are committed to service and value. We offer competitive pricing and exceptional service to every single one of our clients. We also have a quick turnaround lead time. Thank you for your interest and we look forward to working with you!


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with high-quality workmanship, service and stone at the best prices in the industry. Our rigorous quality control and extensive selection of stones has contributed to our tremendous growth and unparalelled customer experience. Customer satisfaction is what we have earned and we are proud of it.

Granite

Granite has been in use for thousands of years as dimension stone, construction material, decorative, and architectural stone.

Quartzite

Quartzite is a hard, non-foliated metamorphic rock which was originally
pure quartz sandstone.


Marbles

Marble is a metamorphic rock composed of recrystallized carbonate minerals, most commonly calcite or dolomite

Quartz

Quartz minerals are mixed with resin and laid out to form slabs.
